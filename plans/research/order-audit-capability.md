# page-foundry v3 companion-capability audit

Method: read `skills/page-foundry/SKILL.md` (companion tables, Phase -1 through 6, integration notes) against the full SKILL.md of each companion at the pinned cache/scratchpad paths. Excluded per instructions: customer-research Mode 2 (already queued). Findings marked **[gap]** (capability left on the table), **[misorder]** (right capability, wrong phase), **[ceremony]** (invoked, adds little).

---

## Per-companion findings

### product-marketing (core, Phase 0)
- **Used well:** auto-draft-from-codebase mode is exactly what Phase 0 step 3 orchestrates (repo README, PRD, site, conversation in; interview only fills gaps). The `.agents/product-marketing.md` path convention matches.
- **[gap] Switching Dynamics and anti-persona are produced but never consumed.** The companion writes Section 8 (JTBD Four Forces: push/pull/habit/anxiety) and Section 7's anti-persona into the brief, but page-foundry's INPUT/OUTPUT row and Phase 1 text name only "positioning, ICP, differentiation, competitive frame, proof inventory." Habit + anxiety are the raw material of the Phase 1 objection map, and the anti-persona is the complement of conversion rule 10 (the red-team read needs to know which "not for me" conclusions are deliberate disqualification vs. defect). Belongs: Phase 1 objection map and Phase 3 red-team read should read those brief sections by name.
- **[gap] Update mode never invoked.** The companion has an explicit existing-doc workflow ("summarize, ask which sections to update"). Phase 0 only branches on absent/present; a second run with `conversion data` and `learnings` in the foundry-log never re-invokes product-marketing to refresh a stale brief. Belongs: Phase 0 step 1 on repeat runs.

### customer-research (core, Phases 0–1)
- **Used well:** Mode 1 asset analysis, verbatim-with-source discipline (page-foundry's Verbatim/Paraphrase split is stricter than the companion's own), two-pass invocation (intake + Phase 1 extension).
- **[gap] The entire synthesis layer is unused.** The companion's core value past quote extraction is: theme clustering, frequency × intensity scoring, confidence labels (High = 3+ independent sources, unprompted), the 12-month recency window, sample-bias checks, and the 5-data-point minimum per segment. page-foundry asks only for "quotes with sources," and the `voc.md` format has no field for theme, confidence, or date-weighting. Consequence: Phase 1 orders the message hierarchy and objection map "in the words of that file" with no mechanism preventing one loud, old, unrepresentative quote from steering the hero. Belongs: Phase 1, before hierarchy/objection ordering — extend `voc.md` (or add `voc-themes.md`) with the companion's ranked-theme synthesis template.
- **[gap] Extraction framework fields (trigger events, JTBD, alternatives-considered) never requested**, though the archetype compiler's five questions (dominant awareness state, buyer entry states) are precisely what trigger-event data answers. Belongs: Phase 0 output feeding the Phase 2 compiler.
- **[gap] Competitive-intelligence deliverable (format #5) not wired as the fallback** when competitor-profiling (enhancer) is declined: Phase 0 falls back to "the conversation and what product-marketing inferred" even though a core, guaranteed-present companion can mine competitor reviews under Mode 1. Belongs: Phase 0 step 3 fallback path. (Adjacent to, but distinct from, the queued Mode 2 finding — this is a routing gap, not an authorization gap.)

### marketing-psychology (core, Phase 1)
- **Used well:** lever selection into `persuasion-map.md` at the right moment (post-VOC, pre-spec), consumed by Phases 2–3.
- **[gap] Invoked at roughly 15% of its catalog.** The phase text's parenthetical ("anchoring, loss aversion, social proof placement, commitment and consistency") names four copy levers; the companion carries five families. Left on the table:
  - **Pricing psychology** (charm vs. round pricing, Rule of 100, good-better-best, decoy, mental accounting) — never reaches the pricing-page/course-sales/membership archetypes. Worse: when the `pricing` enhancer is absent, Phase 2's fallback is "degraded" even though a core companion holds the pricing-psychology rules. Belongs: Phase 2 step 3 on pricing sections.
  - **Design/delivery models** (Hick's law, BJ Fogg B=MAP, activation energy, paradox of choice, goal-gradient) — these are structure levers, not copy levers; the Phase 1 framing ("claim order, objection answers, CTA framing") means nobody ever asks for them when the spec fixes CTA count, form length, and tier count. Belongs: Phase 2 spec compilation.
  - **Pratfall effect** — the designed answer to page-foundry's own thin-proof-inventory situation (integrity rules forbid inventing proof; honest-weakness framing is the ethical substitute). Belongs: Phase 1 when the proof inventory comes back thin.
- **[misorder] (mild):** a single Phase 1 invocation cannot cover structure decisions made in Phase 2; either the persuasion map must explicitly include structural levers or Phase 2 needs a second, scoped invocation.

### cro (core, Phase 2 + Gate 1)
- **Used well:** spec structuring and the independent Gate 1 audit (MECLABS rubric is page-foundry's own, correctly kept as arbiter).
- **[misorder] Existing-page analysis missing from the rewrite entry path.** cro's primary mode is analyzing a live page ("use this even if the user just shares a URL"). page-foundry's own trigger list includes "my landing page is not converting," and Scope notes route rewrites to Phase 1 — but nothing invokes cro on the *current* page to produce the leak diagnosis before re-deriving the message architecture. The companion's highest-leverage mode is skipped on exactly the entry path it was built for. Belongs: rewrite intake, before Phase 1.
- **[gap] `references/form.md` never invoked.** newsletter-capture, waitlist-coming-soon, event-webinar, and campaign-landing are form-centric archetypes; field count, multi-step design, and error handling are first-order conversion factors there, and no phase hands cro the form spec. Belongs: Phase 2 step 3 (archetype-conditional, same pattern as pricing/aso) and Gate 1.
- **[gap] `references/experiments.md` never invoked.** The foundry-log demands conversion data "change a decision or say why not," but no phase generates the test hypotheses that produce that data. cro's experiment-ideas library is the natural Phase 6 output feeding `open items` / next run. Belongs: Phase 6 close.
- **[ceremony] Skeleton pre-read scoring (Phase 2 step 2).** cro's third touch — scoring one-line pre-reads produced from page-foundry's own rubric — adds an invocation whose output the builder could produce from `references/conversion-rules.md`. Thin; the step 3 and Gate 1 invocations carry all the weight.

### copywriting (core, Phase 3)
- **Used well:** hero candidates by framing, headline variant scoring, section-by-section body — the invocation is faithful to the companion's structure and adds discipline (scored candidates file).
- **[gap] Meta content (page title, meta description) never drafted.** The companion explicitly offers "Meta Content: page title (for SEO), meta description" as an output. No phase asks for it, yet Gate 6 checks "meta" at ship time — the exact retrofit-at-gate failure the skill itself documents for the answer block ("a gate can only verify what a spec demanded"). SERP title/description is the page's first headline and currently ships having bypassed the voice gate and `copy-approved.md` entirely. Belongs: Phase 3 step 2, through the voice chain, into the snapshot.

### copy-editing (enhancer, Phase 3)
- **Used well:** positioned correctly (after copywriting's draft), changelog demanded as evidence.
- **[gap] Invoked as a cut-only pass; the Seven Sweeps go unnamed.** "Tighten and cut" maps to maybe two of seven sweeps. "So What" (benefit bridges) and "Specificity" (vague-to-concrete upgrades) are editorial passes nothing else in the pipeline performs mechanically; "Prove It" and "Zero Risk" would pre-empt Gate 8 and conversion-rule findings while copy is still cheap to change. Belongs: Phase 3 step 2, naming the sweeps in the invocation.
- **[gap, judgment call] Expert Panel Scoring unused** — the companion says "Always for launch copy, pricing pages, and high-traffic landing pages," which is page-foundry's entire surface. Partially overlaps the red-team read (which covers entry-state skepticism but not craft scoring). Worth a scoped mention; full iterate-to-8+ would collide with page-foundry's explicit over-processing guardrail, so adopt the panel read once, not the loop.

### site-architecture (not a companion — correctly, mostly)
- Page-foundry ships one page and flags multi-page work as out of scope; excluding this skill is right.
- **[gap, minor]:** (a) the Scope note says "flag site-architecture work separately" but never names this skill as the handoff — one sentence fixes that; (b) the URL-pattern table (`/lp/{slug}`, `/compare/x` vs `/vs/x`) is directly relevant when Phase 2 picks the URL for campaign-landing and comparison-alternatives archetypes, and nothing consults it; (c) the shipped page's inbound-link/no-orphan rule is a natural Gate 6 line for pages entering an existing site.

### ab-testing (not a companion — a real omission)
- **[gap] The measurement loop has no engine.** Gate 7 wires the conversion event; the foundry-log stores `conversion data`; the skill's stated differentiator is the per-property learning loop. But nothing converts a shipped page into a hypothesis ("Because [foundry-log observation], we believe [change] will cause [outcome]..."), an ICE-scored backlog, or a playbook entry — which is ab-testing's whole surface, from the same pinned source. Explore-mode losing variants are also pre-built B-arms nobody offers to actually test on properties with traffic. Belongs: enhancer tier, Phase 6/Gate 7, writing a `test-backlog` block into the foundry-log entry.

### image (not a companion — a real omission)
- **[gap] Static imagery has no owner.** Phase 5 requires "images sized, compressed, lazy-loaded, real alt text" and the integrity rules demand real screenshots over reconstructions — but no companion produces or optimizes them (Remotion owns motion only). Specifically left on the table: OG/social preview image (the image skill calls its absence a top mistake; Gate 6's "meta" line doesn't produce one), the real-screenshot-not-AI-UI rule (independently reinvented by page-foundry's integrity rules — the skills agree and should be wired), device-mockup workflow for product proof, and the WebP/AVIF optimization checklist that is the main Gate 4 performance lever. Belongs: enhancer tier, Phase 5 (asset production) and Gate 6 (OG image), same pinned source.

### impeccable (enhancer, Phases 4–6)
- **Used well:** the deepest integration in the file — PRODUCT.md pre-write to skip the redundant interview, `document`→`detect` as one loop, `palette.mjs` for greenfield seeds, hooks off during runs, merge-never-clobber, `clarify` excluded with a stated reason (no voice enforcement — correct), `init`/`craft`/`shape` implicitly excluded (correct: they are from-scratch build flows that would compete with Phases 2–5, though the notes never say so).
- **[gap] `critique` → `polish` loop unwired.** impeccable's designed pairing is critique writing a scored snapshot that `polish` consumes as its backlog. page-foundry invokes `critique` as advisory and stops: the snapshot lands in `.impeccable/critique/` with no consumer, and the post-failed-render-review fix loop names only "targeted refinement verbs." As wired, critique is ceremony; adding "a failed render review runs `polish` against the critique snapshot" makes both real. Belongs: Gate 5 fix loop.
- **[misorder] `live` slotted at explore mode.** `live` needs a running dev server, but explore mode (Phase 4) produces static hero mockups for a page that does not exist yet — on greenfield builds there is nothing to run `live` against until Phase 5. It fits rewrites (existing page running) or post-build variant iteration. The note should scope it to those cases.
- **[gap, minor] `animate` unnamed at Phase 5.** Remotion owns clips; page-level motion (scroll reveals, hover states) has no owner, and impeccable's animate reference carries the exact rule that protects Gate 2 (reveal animations gated on transitions ship blank in headless renderers — which would also corrupt the verbatim-copy diff). One line at Phase 5 build.
- **[gap, minor] Undocumented exclusions:** `audit` (overlaps Gates 3/4 — defensible, say so), `adapt`/`optimize` as named fix-loop verbs when Gate 3/4 failures are responsive or perf-shaped.
- **[ceremony] `overdrive`.** "Rarely, when the spec's motion slot justifies a technically extraordinary hero" — a vague trigger for a command in direct tension with the performance budget and 5-second-clarity rules on a conversion page. Its table-row listing invites invocations that Gate 4 then fights. Cut it or bind it to an explicit user request.

## Top 10 by page-quality impact

1. **Meta title/description never written under the voice gate** (copywriting, Phase 3). The page's SERP/share headline ships unvoiced and unsnapshotted while Gate 6 "checks meta" it never demanded — the skill's own named anti-pattern.
2. **cro's live-page diagnosis missing from the rewrite entry path** (rewrite intake, pre-Phase 1). The companion's primary mode is skipped on the "my page isn't converting" trigger the skill advertises.
3. **customer-research synthesis layer unused** (Phase 1): no theme ranking, confidence labels, or recency weighting means an outlier quote can steer the hero; quote integrity is mechanical but quote *representativeness* is unchecked.
4. **cro `references/form.md` never invoked on form-centric archetypes** (Phase 2/Gate 1): field-level friction is the dominant leak on newsletter-capture, waitlist, event-webinar, campaign-landing.
5. **No `image` companion** (Phase 5/Gate 6): OG image, real-screenshot proof workflow, and the primary Gate 4 optimization levers have no owner despite three gates touching them.
6. **marketing-psychology scoped to four copy levers** (Phases 1–2): pricing psychology never reaches pricing archetypes (even as the fallback when the `pricing` enhancer is absent), and Fogg/Hick choice architecture never informs the spec.
7. **No `ab-testing` companion and cro's experiments.md unused** (Phase 6/Gate 7): the conversion-data flywheel — the skill's stated differentiator — has measurement but no hypothesis engine.
8. **impeccable `critique`→`polish` unwired** (Gate 5): the scored snapshot has no consumer, making critique ceremony and the render-review fix loop weaker than the companion's designed loop.
9. **copy-editing reduced to a trim pass** (Phase 3): So What and Specificity sweeps are distinct passes nothing else performs; the Expert Panel is a judgment call worth one scoped use.
10. **product-marketing's Switching Dynamics and anti-persona produced but never consumed** (Phase 1 objection map, Phase 3 red-team read): habit/anxiety are objections, and the anti-persona defines which "not for me" is intentional.

**Ceremony to cut or tighten:** impeccable `overdrive` (vague trigger, fights Gate 4), cro's skeleton pre-read scoring (thin third touch), `critique`-without-`polish` (as wired), `live` at explore on greenfield (nothing to attach to).
