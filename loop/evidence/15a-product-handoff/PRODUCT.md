# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Developers and founders who ship many pages: indie hackers, solo founders, small SaaS teams, open-source maintainers, and agencies. The decision-maker is the builder, a practitioner who reads the source before installing. The job: turn a product into a real page fast, and hold one voice and one quality bar across dozens of pages, without the output looking like every other AI page.

## Product Purpose

An open-source Claude Code skill that turns one brief into a finished, high-converting marketing page that does not read or look AI-made. It runs the page through seven build phases and eight ship gates, invoking best-of-breed marketing and design skills as the primary path; a page that fails a gate does not ship. Success is adoption: installs, stars, and real pages in the wild.

## Positioning

For developers and founders who ship many pages, page-foundry is the Claude Code skill that puts a whole marketing team (best-of-breed positioning, copy, conversion, and design skills) on every page and refuses to ship one that reads or looks AI-made, unlike prompting an agent yourself or a templated AI builder.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: install (`npx skills add taylorbanks/page-foundry`). Secondary: star on GitHub.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: one command puts a marketer, a copywriter, a conversion specialist, and a designer on every page you ship, and checks the result so slop cannot ship.
- Belief ladder: (1) one command runs a whole marketing team on every page and gates the result; (2) it runs the best skills that already exist, in order, and tells you when a run is partial; (3) one voice across every page, enforced by a scanner reading a rules file you edit; (4) it will not fake anything: no invented testimonial, number, command, or staged screenshot; (5) the output is yours: static HTML or a design-handoff package, no lock-in.
- Proof on hand: three real pages built with it, clickable and visibly unalike (eatpagosa.com/about, cyberprobono.com, streampatron.org); the author is a security practitioner; the only executable is one standard-library Python scanner. Installs are early and low; show real pages, never a counter.

## Brand Personality

Plain, specific, confident, dry. Honest, technical, opinionated, no-nonsense. Describe the work rather than praising it; concrete nouns and real numbers over adjectives; the brief's own rule, verbatim: `no hype`.

## Anti-references

- Hosted template builders: `"Another AI builder that makes the same template."` is the objection this page must never visually confirm.
- The generic AI page look, and the AI-slop vocabulary and patterns the voice gate blocks. Verbatim buyer language: `"I'm tired of every page looking like the same ai-generated bullshit."`

## Design Principles

- Does not look AI-made.
- One voice at scale.
- Honest by construction.
- You own the output.
